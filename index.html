Wikipedia on your Desktop

Search

Find Wikipedia articles with search suggestions, either from the app or directly on the desktop thanks to the GNOME Shell search integration.

Search Suggestions

With the help of search suggestions it's easy to find the article you are looking for.

Search Text in Articles

Thanks to the integrated text search you can quickly find any content within the article.

View

Open multiple articles in tabs and read them with a simpler and distraction-free view. Customize fonts and easily switch between light, sepia and dark mode.

Multiple Languages

Wike supports searching and reading articles in more than 300 languages.

Select the languages ​​you want to make available for searching and reading articles.

Press the search settings button in the entry to choose the active language.

You can view an article in other languages ​​by clicking the languages button in the sidebar.
